About Lefta

Lefta is a personal finance management platform built to help individuals take full control of their money — whether you're tracking daily expenses, planning a monthly budget, or working toward a savings goal.

We believe managing money shouldn't be complicated. That's why we built Lefta to be clean, intuitive, and accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

To empower every individual with simple, powerful tools that turn financial stress into financial confidence.
